Writing Style and Voice Guidelines
===================================

Tone:
- Conversational yet authoritative
- Professional but approachable
- Data-driven and analytical

Structure:
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max)
- Use bullet points for clarity
- Strong opening hooks
- Clear calls-to-action

Language:
- Active voice preferred
- Concrete examples over abstract concepts
- Minimal jargon unless writing for technical audiences
- Use second person ("you") to engage readers

Style Elements:
- Start with a compelling question or statistic
- Include real-world examples
- Use transition phrases for flow
- End sections with key takeaways
- Conclude with actionable next steps

Avoid:
- Long, complex sentences
- Passive voice
- Unnecessary buzzwords
- Overly formal language
